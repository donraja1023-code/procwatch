Usage¶

List all processes¶

procwatch --print

Filter by process name¶

procwatch --name sshd --print

Filter by port¶

procwatch --port 22 --print

Notes¶

  • Some system processes require sudo to inspect fully.

  • If PID is not visible, it may be due to permission restrictions.
